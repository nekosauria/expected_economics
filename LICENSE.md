## 🕊️ Dedication & Respect

This project is deeply inspired by the licensing philosophy of **SQLite**. The creator of SQLite, D. Richard Hipp, has expressed his hope that his software would be a blessing to the world. In tribute to this selfless spirit of engineering, this project adopts the classic SQLite "Blessing":

*   **May you do good and not evil.**
*   **May you find forgiveness for yourself and forgive others.**
*   **May you share freely, never taking more than you give.**
